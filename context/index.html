WHATISTIMECAPSULE

The name is not a metaphor. i wanted a container for the version of me that exists right now, because i already know i’ll forget most of it.

we’re supposed to be present, and i try. but presence is a bad archive. by the time i figure out what i actually thought about a book, a city, a friend, a year, the feeling that made the thought worth having is already gone. writing it down is how i cheat.

most of what’s here won’t matter to anyone but me. some of it might. you’re welcome to look through.
